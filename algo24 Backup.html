Exercise sheets and classes

After each exercise class, a new exercise sheet will be made available below, except for the first one which will be made available a bit later. You have one week to solve as many exercises as you can. However, there is no need to write down your solutions for handing them in (but making thorough notes of your solutions is recommended). Instead, at the beginning of each exercise class you may indicate which problems on the sheet you have solved. Then students will be chosen by chance to present their solutions. Points are not awarded for presented solutions that turn out to be incorrect or incomplete.
